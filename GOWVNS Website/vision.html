Vision (Mission)

By way of this News Syndicate, we are catering to associate the world with positive journalism through active support of the enthusiastic people. Recent drastic developments in the field of journalism have posed grave new challenges before the up-coming journalists. They are finding hard to cope with emerging situation, merely through help of old and stale strategies. Moreover, when the society itself is experiencing changes and upheavals in many aspects, the change in the role of journalists is also inevitable. It is the need of the hour to take effective steps in the creative direction, to meet new challenges. All this marks the essence of the formation of a common platform named as the Group of World Vision News Services (GOWVNS). The group is constituted of energetic people who are well experienced and skilled in running all the facets of the media, including the information and the publicity.
